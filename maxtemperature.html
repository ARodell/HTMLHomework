Summary of City Latitude vs. Maximum Temperature

These data show us that generally cities that are locarted closer to the equator were experiencing higher maximum temperatures during the month of January in 2017 than cities that have distances further away from the equator. Also, cities located south of the equator had higher maximum temperatures during this time of year than cities located north of the equator.
